Beats in Bytes


Arin Khare has experience in data science and statistics, specializing in the Python programming language. He has written code for automating statistical tasks including regression, statistical tests, finding measures of center and variation, and creating graphs. He is currently in the process of developing a midi-to-kern file converter in Python. To contact, please email +arinmkhare@gmail.com.

He conducted all the data collection for the BiB project, and has created a powerful analysis website from scratch. He has experience in Bash programming, web development, and statistics, and in the future hopes to expand this project. He can be found at +kaivernooy@gmail.com.

James Lian conducted an integral part of the research for the BiB project: gathering and preparing files. While analyzing data, he did correlation and geographic analysis. He has experience in statistics and math and in the future is interested in expanding his skills in programming. He is currently assisting Kai the design of the website. You can contact him at +jamesanxinlian@gmail.com.
